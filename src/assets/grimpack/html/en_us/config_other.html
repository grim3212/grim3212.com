Other ways to configure GrimPack are modifying any other values. Most follow a specific type and will tell you what it expects.

B:"Generate Element 115"=true
D:"Grenade Launcher Explosion Strength"=4.0
I:"Multitool durability multiplier"=3

  • B: Expects a boolean (true or false)
  • I: Expects an integer, so no decimal in the range of '-2147483647' - '2147483647'
  • D: Expects a double, so can contain a decimal in the range of '-1.7976931348623157E308' - '1.7976931348623157E308'
  • S: Expects a string

Other ways you are able to modify GrimPack are by creating a ResourcePack.
